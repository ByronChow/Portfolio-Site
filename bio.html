ABOUT
Byron Chow is an artist and designer raised in New York City. Spending a majority of his childhood traversing the streets of SoHo, he has used his early interest in fashion, street art and brand identity as focal themes within his work. Other interests include philosophy, web design and thrift stores. He is a recent graduate from the Rhode Island School of Design, and received an honors BFA in Graphic Design.
